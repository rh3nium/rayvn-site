About Rayvn Tech: Robotics for Resilient Communities

Rayvn Tech was founded on the belief that cutting-edge robotics and AI should be leveraged to protect human life and build resilient communities in the face of disaster. Our journey began with a focus on solving the critical problem of communication blackouts and inaccessible debris fields following catastrophic events.

We combine deep expertise in swarm robotics, computer vision, and secure mesh networking to deliver the most robust autonomous rescue system available.
